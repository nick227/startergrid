Marketplaces set minimum photo counts and content rules. This system enforces configured minimums before dispatch; platforms may enforce stricter rules on receipt.

## Typical minimums

| Tier | Common rule |
| --- | --- |
| Internal validation | 4–6 images to leave blocked state |
| Major classified | 8–12 exterior/interior mix |
| OEM programs | No watermarks, neutral background, no stock art |

## Recon workflow

Units in recon often have title scans or one lane photo. They remain **blocked** until lane or photographer loads the set. Price and VIN can be correct while photos still fail.

## Order

Some feeds send primary photo first. Reorder in Inventory when the hero shot should be front 3/4, not stock label image.

## Example

Six photos pass internal check; OEM feed rejects for seventh required interior dash image. Row may show warning here but fail on that platform until seventh image is added.
