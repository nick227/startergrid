# Segment: Buy Here Pay Here Dealer

## Buyer

BHPH owner/operator with used inventory, payments, forms, and customer follow-up already consuming attention.

## Real Pain

- wants leads but cannot let marketing create chaos
- vehicle data exists inside BHPH/DMS tools but may not be clean for ad/catalog platforms
- photos and prices change often
- sold/removed vehicles must disappear quickly
- finance workflows are important but not the first Launchpad wedge

## Best Hook

```txt
We separate launch readiness from finance operations: clean inventory pages, clear lead capture, and platform packets without replacing your BHPH system.
```

## First Offer

```txt
Launch Audit + Storefront Readiness Setup
$299 audit credited toward $799 setup
```

Why:

BHPH dealers often need discovery before implementation because workflows vary widely.

## What To Emphasize

- not replacing payment/accounting tools
- not touching lender/compliance workflows at MVP
- sold/removed vehicle propagation
- lead capture with vehicle interest
- clean storefront as controlled demand source

## Discovery Questions

- Which system tracks payments and customer accounts?
- Where does inventory originate?
- How often do prices or availability change?
- Do you advertise payment terms publicly?
- Are lead forms going to sales, collections, or owner inbox?
- Which channels have caused bad leads or bad-fit buyers?

## Objections

"We need financing built in."

```txt
That is a later integration. First we make your inventory and lead path clean without disrupting the finance system you already rely on.
```

"Our inventory changes too fast."

```txt
That is exactly why readiness and update propagation matter. Price change, sold, removed, and photo changes need tracked channel actions.
```

## Disqualify If

- wants payment servicing replacement
- wants credit/lender routing now
- cannot expose current inventory
- cannot support lead follow-up
